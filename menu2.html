Ingredients

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 2 cups water
  • 500g beef, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt and spices to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pot and gradually add maize flour while stirring.
  2. Continue stirring until thick, then let it cook on low heat for 5 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry onions until golden brown.
  4. Add diced beef and cook until browned.
  5. Add tomatoes, bell pepper, and spices, then simmer until beef is tender.
  6. Serve the beef stew with hot ugali.

Nutritional Information

Rich in protein, carbohydrates, and essential vitamins.
