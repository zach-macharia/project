Ingredients

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup dried omena
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pot and gradually add maize flour while stirring.
  2. Continue stirring until thick, then let it cook on low heat for 5 minutes.
  3. Wash the omena in warm water and drain.
  4. Fry onions in cooking oil until golden brown.
  5. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  6. Add the omena, stir, and let it cook for 10 minutes.
  7. Serve the omena with hot ugali.

Nutritional Information

Rich in protein, carbohydrates, and essential vitamins.
